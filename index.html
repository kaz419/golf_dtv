Ken Sugiyama is the driving force behind Ken's Golf. Born in Japan and raised in Thailand since the age of 2, Ken brings a unique international perspective to the game. He honed his professional skills at the prestigious Mizuho High School golf club in Ibaraki, Japan. To ensure a welcoming experience for everyone, Ken can speak 4 languages. His goal is to combine his training with a friendly, accessible approach for golfers of all backgrounds and levels.
